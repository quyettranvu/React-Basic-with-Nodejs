Welcome to my page and thank you very much for visiting here! My name is Quyet and I will tell you how to use my page: -You can register new account in the Signup page and after that login to your page. -When you register you need to give your ID number, first name, last name and password, your password needs to have numbers and letters alternate and start from number. -After signing up, you can login into your account and see your informations about your account on main page. -You can update your information with the button "Edit", if you want to change password, remember to input old password correctly! -By clicking into my avatar, you can see informations about me, about my tasks and this instruction. -If you want to log out my page, just click into the button "Log out". -Thank you once again for reading my instruction about user, checkout other instructions in other slides ^^!
